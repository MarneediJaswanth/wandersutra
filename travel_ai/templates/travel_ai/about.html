About WanderSutra

WanderSutra is an intelligent AI-powered travel planning system designed to provide personalized travel predictions, destination insights, weather intelligence, budget estimation, and route analysis for travelers across India.

Project Overview

The system combines Machine Learning, intelligent prediction models, geographic analysis, and weather-based decision making to help users plan smarter and more efficient journeys.

WanderSutra analyzes travel conditions, seasonal patterns, estimated costs, travel duration, transportation methods, and traveler preferences to generate highly personalized travel recommendations.

Core Features

Smart Destination Planning

Predicts suitable travel destinations based on user preferences, travel month, and budget.

AI-Based Cost Prediction

Estimates hotel, food, and transportation costs using machine learning models.

Weather Intelligence

Provides weather forecasts including temperature, humidity, and rainfall conditions.

Route & Travel Analysis

Calculates travel duration, seasonal suitability, and transportation recommendations.

Interactive Visualization

Displays maps, charts, and analytical insights for better travel understanding.

Personalized AI Insights

Generates intelligent travel insights based on prediction confidence and user selections.
